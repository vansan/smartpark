POC and MVP are often confused—but they’re not the same thing, and using the wrong one at the wrong time can waste effort.

❌ First — Clear Misconception
POC ≠ MVP

If you treat them the same, you’ll either:

Overbuild too early
OR

Under-validate critical tech risks

🧠 Simple Difference (In Your Context)
🧪 POC (Proof of Concept)
Goal:
👉 “Can this idea technically work?”

Internal use only

Rough / ugly UI

No scalability

Focus on hardest parts

🚀 MVP (Minimum Viable Product)
Goal:
👉 “Can users actually use & pay for this?”

Real users

Clean UI

End-to-end flow

Deployable

🅿️ In Your SmartPark Case
Let’s apply this directly.
